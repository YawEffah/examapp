- About Exam+ -


Exam+ is aimed at enhancing the efficiency, accuracy, and security of the examination process by providing a streamlined and automated solution for reporting malpractices, verifying registered candidates and their respective exam halls.

Objective

The primary objective of the system is to simplify the examination process and eliminate manual errors associated with candidate and exam hall verification.

Key Features

The system enables registered candidates to verify their registration status by entering their student ID. It will cross-check the information against the university's database and provide an instant confirmation of their registration status and the respective exam hall allocated to them. The system will ensure equitable distribution and optimal utilization of exam halls.

The system enables examinations officers to efficiently record details of any student or exam officer engaged in examination malpractice. The recorded information can be submitted via the system to the respective authorities.

The system provides real-time updates on the availability and occupancy of exam halls. This will allow administrative staff to monitor the occupancy levels, make informed decisions regarding hall allocation, and avoid overcrowding.
